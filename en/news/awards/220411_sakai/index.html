Dr. Sakai won the Flash Talk Award at QM2022!


We are happy to congratulate Dr. Sakai, a postdoc in our group! She won the Flash Talk Award at the Quark Matter (QM) 2022 (29th International Conference on Ultra-relativistic Nucleus-Nucleus Collisions) international conference.

Details

Award: Best poster

Poster Title: Space-time evolution of critical fluctuations in an expanding system

Location: KRAKÓW, Poland (Hybrid)

Date 2022, April 4th - 10th

Sakai san, congratulations!
