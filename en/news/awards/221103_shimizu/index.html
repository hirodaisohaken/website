Dr. Yusuke Shimizu's paper got most cite paper at PTEP


We are happy to congratulate Dr. Yusuke Shimizu, a staff in our group! He and his collaborators’ paper got the most cite paper at Progress of Theoretical and Experimental Physics (PTEP) journal.

Details

Award: Most cite Paper

Paper Title: Modular S3-invariant flavor model in SU(5) grand unified theory

Journal: Progress of Theoretical and Experimental Physics (PTEP)

Shimizu san, congratulations!
