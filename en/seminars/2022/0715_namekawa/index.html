Sohaken Seminar: Yusuke Namekawa (Hiroshima Univ.)


  • Speaker: Yusuke Namekawa (Hiroshima Univ.)
  • Date: Friday 07/15 from 14:35 JST B305 and Online(Teams)
  • Title: Lattice simulation for system with sign problem
  • Abstract: Recently, several methods have been developed to overcome the sign problem, which appears in the system of a complex action and prevents us from a quantitative study by traditional Monte Carlo simulations. In this talk, I will explain our attempt especially for QCD at finite density and system with a complex coupling.
