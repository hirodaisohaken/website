On Saturday, June 6th, a group of about a dozen of us went on a field trip to Mazda Stadium to watch the interleague game between the Hiroshima Carp and the Orix Buffaloes. The Carp maintained the lead from the beginning, but in the eighth inning they were in danger of being overtaken. However, some great plays by Taka and Nahara helped turn the tide, and the Carp won 7-4. After the game, we had a celebratory meal.
